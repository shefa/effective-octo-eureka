Few Words About Us

VIPCONSULT DB LTD is an accounting and consulting company, situated in Varna, Bulgaria. It delivers a range of financial, legal and assurance services. What distinguishes our firm is its persistency in guaranteeing punctual scrupulous reports, and most of all the personal advisory sessions we offer to our clients.

We have always measured our success with our clients’ fulfillment with the services provided. A well done job means that the specific, personal advisory strategies implemented, as well as the immaculate bookkeeping we provide, have optimized the decision-making processes a firm faces, so that it maintains its stability and facilitates further growth.
